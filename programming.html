About ChatBot

ChatBot

ChatBot was my first video game I made by myself, I was inspired by artificial intelligence and the fears about it. The player tries to escape the game with obstacles and dialog. I designed the art and programmed it myself using construct 3.

About Lovin Hell

Lovin' hell

I also made a game called Lovin’ Hell which was a project I did for a game jam. I was the programmer and had a lot of fun making the game even though it was a little difficult. The game was a dating sim where you can date the Grim Reaper. We used a game engine that wasn't known for those types of games so I pretty much had to figure it out myself.

About Bellaby's dungeon escape

One of my projects was Bellaby’s Dungeon Escape. I worked on it from March-June 2025. We only had one month to make the game, which made it difficult for my team since we failed to communicate and had two artists with two different ideas of what to make. During that time, I was the only programmer and writer on the project so I had to do a lot of the programming by myself. Bellaby's dungeon escape was my final group project for my first year of gaming and multimedia. It was posted on itch.io on 6/10/2025
