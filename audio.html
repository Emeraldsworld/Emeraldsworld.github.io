Audio

Class audios:

This is an audio I made for school and was my first audio project. It is supposed to seem creepy. I used static noises and creaking sounds to sound like someone was running. I used rain sounds to make it sound like they went outdoors. I made it using Reaper and freesounds.com

This was a project about making a story only using sounds. I made it seem creepy and unsettling. I was inspired by five nights at Freddy's and the listener is being chased by a robot what is possessed by ghosts of children. I made it using Reaper and freesounds.com

This was a project where I spotted and redesigned audio for game play in animal crossing. I used reaper and freesounds.com. You are digging and mining rocks. You get a few coins and ores. then you cover the holes back up with dirt.

This was a project I made for school where I turned my friends voice into a monster voice. The character he is voicing is a character in one of my games called "Natalie vs. the mind" but it didn't make it because we decided to not use audio for our project. In the audio, I did 3 different versions of the monster voice and made it overlap to give the illusion of intrusive thoughts.
